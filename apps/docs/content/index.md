### 6. Input World and Story Settings
![Step 6 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/77ec9df8-173d-4263-bc42-10ae4915fdb2/steps/d5fb4889-86a4-4c9f-926c-ba46f19d3ad3/10061c3a-ba6f-4ad9-9da1-f0a7eea3adbc.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5002&fp-y=0.2289&fp-z=1.0212&w=1200&border=2%2CF4F2F7&border-radius=8%2C8%2C8%2C8&border-radius-inner=8%2C8%2C8%2C8&blend-align=bottom&blend-mode=normal&blend-x=0&blend-w=1200&blend64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL21hZGUtd2l0aC10YW5nby13YXRlcm1hcmstdjIucG5n&mark-x=13&mark-y=118&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTYlMkNGRjc0NDImdz0xMTc1Jmg9OTUmZml0PWNyb3AmY29ybmVyLXJhZGl1cz0xMA%3D%3D)
After clicking, the world building interface will expand at the top.
*   **Prompt / Command**: You can enter commands, such as "I want a DND-style Gilgamesh adventure story".
*   **Reference Files**: You can upload materials or create character cards based on an existing Lorebook. Click **Reference Files** or **Lorebook** in the bottom left to operate.
*   **Game Modes**:
    *   **Narrative**: Ideal for a narrative-driven experience, similar to a novel generator.
    *   **Stats-driven**: Preference for stats-driven experience, similar to a DND tabletop game.

Below the world generator, we divide the world state into the following categories. The system will generate them in the order of "Outline -> Opening Story -> World Entity -> Regions -> Organizations -> Characters":
1.  **Opening Story**
2.  **World Entity**
3.  **Regions**
4.  **Organizations**
5.  **Creatures**: Protagonist and other characters
6.  **Story History**: Save history

### 19. Story Timeline
![branch](https://hackmd.io/_uploads/HkJJQuY9bg.jpg)
The Story Timeline in the sidebar provides Checkpoint functionality.
Every choice you make in the game is recorded and stored locally. It supports branch story management: for example, after choosing A at checkpoint 1 and playing for a round, you can switch back to checkpoint 1 and choose B. You can click on any node on the timeline to freely switch to the corresponding checkpoint to continue the game.
